About Khabar e Khail

Khabar e Khail is your premier destination for all things gaming in Pakistan. Founded in 2024, we are passionate about bringing the latest news, reviews, and previews to our gaming community.

Our team of dedicated gamers and writers work tirelessly to provide you with accurate, engaging, and timely content. From mobile gaming trends to eSports tournaments, we cover it all.

Our Mission

At Khabar e Khail, our mission is to:

  • Provide unbiased and comprehensive game reviews
  • Keep our readers informed about the latest gaming news and trends
  • Support and promote the growing gaming industry in Pakistan
  • Foster a vibrant community of gamers across the country
